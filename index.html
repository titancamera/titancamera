Scientist Crate

  • Scientist Cameraman, with a 70% chance of being summoned, a money farming unit.
  • Large Scientist Cameraman, with a 29% chance of being summoned, a base healing unit.
  • Engineer Cameraman, with a 1% chance of being summoned, a minion spawning unit.

Spider Crate

  • Speaker Spider, with a 70% of appearing, a weaker AOE Spider
  • Camera Spider, with a 29.9% of appearing, a high DPS Spider
  • Spider TV, with a 0.1% of appearing, a high DPS AOE Spider

Scary Crate

  • Scary Speakerman, with a 50% chance of being summoned, an Uncommon early game unit with a secondary AOE effect
  • Monster Speakerman, with a 40% chance of being summoned, a Rare AOE unit
  • Pumpkin Farmer, with a 9% (even though the shop says 10%) chance of being summoned, an Epic money farming unit
  • Sinister Cameraman, with a 0.9% chance of being summoned, a Legendary high DPS unit
  • Corrupted Cameraman, with a 0.1% chance of being summoned, a Mythic with the highest DPS in the game for a cheaper price

Turkey Crate

  • Turkey Speakerman, with a 50% chance of being summoned, an Rare AoE unit.
  • Turkey Cameraman , with a 40% chance of being summoned, an Epic, cheap, single target high DPS unit.
  • Large Turkey Speakerman, with a 9% chance of being summoned, a Legendary AoE unit.
  • Chef TV Man, with a 1% chance of being summoned, a Mythic splash damage unit.

Santa Crate

  • Reindeer Speakerman, with a 50% chance of being summoned, an Epic high DPS AOE but expensive unit.
  • Elf Camerawoman, with a 40% chance of being summoned, an early-mid game high DPS Legendary unit.
  • Santa TV Man, with a 9% chance of being summoned, a Mythic money farming unit.
  • Titan Present Man, with a 1% chance of being summoned, a Godly high DPS and splash ranged damage unit.
